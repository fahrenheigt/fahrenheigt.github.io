Welcome to
League of Legends

Lol is a fast-paced, competitive online game developed by Riot Games. Since its release in 2009, it has become one of the most popular MOBA games in the world, offering intense strategy, teamplay, and action-packed combat.

In League of Legends, players assume the role of champions—powerful characters with unique abilities—working together in teams of five to destroy the opposing team’s Nexus, a critical structure located in their base. The game is known for its depth, where mastering champions, team strategies, and map awareness are crucial to success.

What sets League of Legends apart is its diverse roster of over 160 champions, each with distinct playstyles, strengths, and weaknesses. Whether you prefer the role of a damage-dealing assassin, a resilient tank, or a masterful support, there’s a champion for every player.

Join the millions of players worldwide who are part of this dynamic and ever-evolving universe. Whether you're looking to climb the ranked ladder, play casually with friends, or explore the lore behind the champions, League of Legends offers an experience that’s both challenging and incredibly rewarding.

    Types

    There are 23 types of champion statistics divided into 3 different categories: Defensive, Offensive, and Utility. Below are the basic attributes of each statistic.

    Defensive

    • Health [HP]: A champion dies when their health is reduced to zero. Some abilities and effects may scale off health (current, bonus, missing, or maximum).
    • Health regeneration [HP5]: The amount of health a champion restores passively every 5 seconds.
    • Heal and shield power: Increases the strength of healing or shielding effects on yourself or allies.
    • Armor [AR]: Reduces the amount of physical damage taken.
    • Magic resistance [MR]: Reduces the amount of magic damage taken.
    • Tenacity: Reduces the duration of most crowd control effects.
    • Slow resist: Reduces the strength of incoming slows.

    Offensive

    • Attack speed [AS]: The number of basic attacks a champion can perform per second.
    • Attack damage [AD]: Basic attacks deal exactly this amount of damage.
    • Ability power [AP]: Used to amplify abilities and some other damage sources.
    • Critical strike chance [Crit%]: The chance that a basic attack will critically strike.
    • Critical strike damage: The damage dealt when a critical strike occurs.
    • Armor penetration: Ignores a portion of enemy armor when dealing physical damage.
    • Magic penetration: Ignores a portion of enemy magic resistance when dealing magic damage.
    • Life steal: Restores health as a percentage of the damage dealt by basic attacks.
    • Physical vamp: Restores health as a percentage of all physical damage dealt.
    • Omnivamp: Restores health as a percentage of all damage dealt.

    Utility

    • Ability haste [AH]: Reduces the cooldown of abilities.
    • Mana [MP]: A resource that is used to cast abilities.
    • Mana regeneration [MP5]: The amount of mana a champion restores passively every 5 seconds.
    • Energy: A short-term resource for casting abilities, replenishes quickly.
    • Energy regeneration: The amount of energy a champion restores passively every 5 seconds.
    • Attack range: The maximum distance from which a champion can perform a basic attack.
    • Movement speed [MS]: How fast a champion moves across the map.
    • Gold generation: The rate at which a champion passively generates gold.

    Increasing Statistics

    There are eight base statistics that can increase through growth: attack damage, attack speed, armor, magic resistance, health, health regeneration, mana, and mana regeneration. Growth is non-linear and increases with champion level.

    Formula for Champion Statistics at a Specific Level

    Statistic = base + bonus + g × (n - 1) × (0.7025 + 0.0175 × (n - 1))
    base = initial statistic value
    bonus = bonus statistic from any source (runes, items, buffs, etc.)
    g = growth statistic
    n = current champion level
    (n - 1) = total number of level ups

    Example: Alistar starts at level 1 with 685 health. His health growth is 120. At level 2:

    Health = 685 + 120 × (2 - 1) × (0.7025 + 0.0175 × (2 - 1))
    Health = 771.4 (rounded to 771 in game)

    Attack Speed Calculations

    Attack speed calculations use base attack speed, an attack speed ratio, and bonus attack speed percentages. The formula to calculate total attack speed is:

    Total Attack Speed = baseAs + [bonusAs + g × (n - 1) × (0.7025 + 0.0175 × (n - 1))] × AsRatio
    baseAs = base attack speed
    AsRatio = attack speed ratio
    g = percent bonus attack speed growth from leveling
    bonusAs = sum of bonus attack speed from other sources
    n = current champion level

    Example: Volibear at level 3 with his passive fully stacked:

    Total Attack Speed = 0.625 + [0.25 + 0.02 × (3 - 1) × (0.7025 + 0.0175 × (3 - 1))] × 0.7
    Total Attack Speed = 0.625 + 0.19565 = 0.82065
